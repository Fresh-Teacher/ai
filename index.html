Examples

What is the capital of France?

Who won the Nobel Prize for Literature in 2021?

What is the latest smartphone from Apple?

Limitations

My training data only goes up to 2021, so I may not have information on recent events.

I may struggle to understand context and may not provide accurate answers for questions that are too vague or open-ended.

Capabilities

I can answer a wide range of questions on various topics such as history, science, current events, and more.

I can also generate creative writing and perform arithmetic calculations.
